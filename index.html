LOVE YOUR ENEMY AND NEIGHBOR AS YOURSELF

Thou shalt love your neighbor and hate your enemy? Matthew 5:43-44, this means we should love others and that means even loving those who are unkind to us as well as easy to love doesn't mean we shouldn't love them. Because life life is too short, we don't know what will happen to us in the future, only the Lord does. It's better that we will vanish without resentment and hostility.
